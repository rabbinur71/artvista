Whispers of Dawn

Evelyn Hamilton

Whispers of Dawn is a beautifully textured painting that captures the serene elegance of flying birds against a radiant sunrise. The soft blues and warm gold tones flow together, suggesting the early light of dawn breaking over a tranquil landscape. The brushstrokes are expressive and layered, adding depth and movement to the scene and evoking a peaceful and almost dreamlike atmosphere.

Type: Unique Work

Category: Paintings

Size: 100 x 120 cm (hxb)

Material: Acrylic, modeling paste, gesso

Surface: High quality linen canvas on aluminum frame

Edition: 1

Availability: Available

Sides: Painted dark brown/red
