Our Artistic Journey

Curating Extraordinary Encounters

Founded in 2015, ArtVista Gallery emerged from a shared passion for creating meaningful connections between artists and art lovers. We believe every piece tells a story, and every collector becomes part of that narrative.

Our mission is to showcase exceptional contemporary art while preserving the intimate experience of discovery. Unlike traditional galleries, we focus on creating immersive environments where art can be appreciated in dialogue with space, light, and context.

Milestones

2015 - Humble Beginnings

Opened our first 800 sq ft space in downtown with just 3 represented artists. Hosted the groundbreaking "New Perspectives" exhibition.

2017 - First International Show

Curated "Borderless Visions" featuring 12 artists from 6 countries. This exhibition put us on the international art map.

2019 - Expansion

Moved to our current 5,000 sq ft flagship location with dedicated spaces for installations and experimental media.

2022 - Digital Innovation

Launched our virtual gallery platform, making our collections accessible worldwide while maintaining our curated approach.

2025 - Present Day

Representing 47 exceptional artists from 18 countries, with exhibitions rotating quarterly across our physical and digital spaces.
